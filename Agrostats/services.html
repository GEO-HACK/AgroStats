Our Services

Tracking

Our Tracking service empowers farmers with comprehensive tools to monitor and record all agricultural activities. Using GPS field mapping, real-time weather data, and pest monitoring, farmers can maintain detailed logs, analyze progress, and make informed decisions. This service enhances productivity, supports sustainable practices, and ensures efficient resource management, ultimately leading to higher yields and cost savings.

Compliance

Our Project aims at helping farmers meet regulatory requirements and industry standards through robust tracking and comprehensive record-keeping via the following channels: keeping detailed logs of a farmer's agricultural activities, providing alerts and notifications on any ongoing certification/training programs, providing comprehensive reports based on the users data that can be used as audit trails to ensure accountability.

Verification

As much as AgroStats focuses on providing farmer's with tools to record and store their real time farm data, we also aim at linking them with compliance standards from various certification issuing bodies to ensure that the data provided in our application is accurate and can be reviewed to assess that the farmers has met the required standards.

Networking

We aim at intergrating community forums where farmers can discuss common challenges and experiences ,Hosting networking events and webinars that will be for training farmers on the certification and approval process and on matters dealing with usage of the app and advisory services that will provide access to experts in the agricultural sector to provide mentorship and information platforms to the farmers.
